Dial Duel

Jun 1, 2016

Dial Duel is a game about rapid hacking. It is prototyped through a terminal and communication is done through a secure tunnel. Bittish is used as the language very naturally in order to issue commands/actions.

The languages used in the Dial Duel project are both Bittish the natural language and Bittish the PL when it is finished. The language may also be featured in Beln.

Bittish

This combines natural (human) language with concepts from informatics (computer science). It is to be a fully functional constructed language in which grammatical, semantic, and orthographic concepts are represented as sequences of discrete signals.

2010 Dec 25

Both a natural language translator (with Markov chains) and compiler for Bittish are planned for development. Lexing Bittish is subject to any chosen method of orthography. Parsing will be handled according to the central language definition maintained here. Additionally, this site will host a dictionary containing the minimum number of words for a functioning language. (In English, this has been quoted at 2,000.) Bittish is featured in Beln and in Dial Duel.
